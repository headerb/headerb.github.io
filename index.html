ABOUT US

CSUCI Baseball is currently paving the path for current and future students interested in playing baseball at CI. We are looking to compete this season and make a statement to the rest of our league. We are a first year club team, and are building our program from the ground up. We are apart of the Pacific Central Conference in the NCBA Division II. We have 5 series on our schedule this year.
